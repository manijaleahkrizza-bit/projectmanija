Must-Visit Gems

6 Famous Places in Northern Mindanao

Experience the diverse beauty of Northern Mindanao — from white sand beaches to majestic waterfalls and cultural treasures.

Camiguin Island

The "Island Born of Fire" with 7 volcanoes, hot springs, white sand bars, and the iconic Sunken Cemetery. A paradise for nature lovers and history enthusiasts.

Camiguin Province

Maria Cristina Falls

The "Mother of All Falls" and the Philippines' most powerful waterfall. A majestic 320-foot twin cascade that powers the region's hydroelectric plant.

Iligan City, Lanao del Norte

Dahilayan Adventure Park

Home to the longest dual zipline in Asia. Experience thrilling adventures amidst the cool climate of Bukidnon's pine forest mountains.

Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon

White Island

A stunning sandbar off the coast of Camiguin with powdery white sand and breathtaking views of Mt. Hibok-Hibok. Perfect for sunrise viewing and swimming.

Mambajao, Camiguin

Mt. Hibok-Hibok

An active volcano and popular trekking destination offering panoramic views of Camiguin and neighboring islands. A challenging hike with rewarding scenery.

Camiguin Province

Tinago Falls

A hidden waterfall tucked away in a deep ravine. The name "Tinago" means hidden, and visitors descend over 300 steps to reach this emerald pool paradise.

Iligan City, Lanao del Norte
